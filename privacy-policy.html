Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Summary: PromptBook stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to any of your prompts or personal information.

1. Introduction

PromptBook ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users save and organize AI prompts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

PromptBook operates entirely locally on your device. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

3. Data Storage

All data you create in PromptBook is stored locally using Chrome's Storage API:

  • Prompts: Stored locally in your browser
  • Settings: Stored locally in your browser
  • Categories & Tags: Stored locally in your browser

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the Export feature.

4. Permissions Explained

PromptBook requires certain browser permissions to function. Here's why:

  • storage: To save your prompts locally in Chrome's storage
  • activeTab: To detect which AI website you're on and inject the Save button
  • clipboardWrite: To copy prompts to your clipboard when you click "Copy"
  • Host permissions (AI websites): To inject the Save button on supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, NotebookLM, Perplexity)

5. Third-Party Services

PromptBook does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The extension operates completely standalone.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security
  • Only you have access to your saved prompts
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

7. Data Export & Deletion

Export: You can export all your prompts as a JSON file at any time through the Settings menu.

Deletion: You can delete individual prompts or uninstall the extension to remove all data.

8. Children's Privacy

PromptBook does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension does not collect any personal information from any users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
